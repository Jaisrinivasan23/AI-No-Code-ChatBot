Codeathon Rules and Regulations
1. Reporting Time: All participants must report by 9:00 AM. Late arrivals may result in disqualification.

2. Inauguration Function: The inauguration ceremony will take place from 9:30 AM to 10:15 AM. All participants are expected to be present.

3. Event Start: The actual coding event will commence at 10:40 AM. 

4. Lunch Break: A scheduled lunch break will occur from 12:30 PM to 1:00 PM. Please be punctual in returning to the event.

5. Review Sessions: 

Review 1 :  11:30 AM to 12.30 PM. 

Review 2 :  02:00 PM to 03.00 PM. 

Be prepared to showcase your progress during this time.


6. Award Distribution: Awards will be distributed from 3:00 PM to 3:30 PM. All participants are encouraged to attend.


Project Guidelines

Prebuilt Projects : Both hardware and software projects that were prebuilt are allowed. However, modifications and enhancements made during the event will be encouraged.

Additional Notes

Participants are encouraged to work collaboratively but should adhere to the rules regarding originality and project ownership.

Please keep your workspace clean and respect others' space and materials.

Follow all safety protocols, especially for hardware projects.

Have fun and enjoy the experience!


For any questions or clarifications, please contact the event organizers. Enjoy Codeathon Stars!
